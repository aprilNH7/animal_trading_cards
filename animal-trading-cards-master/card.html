Cheetah

Cheetahs are known as the fastest land animals in the world!

  • Scientific Name: Acinonyx jubatus
  • Average Length: 3.5-5 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 12 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands, open plains, dry and scrub forests

Identified commonly by their beautiful black marks ranging from the inner eye down to the outside of mouth. Considered a large cat with a dotted coat that covers their entire body. They are able to enhance their grip when digging their claws into the ground at high speed. Their long tail allows them maneuver and change direction quickly.
